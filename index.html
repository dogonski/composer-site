I work on both acoustic and computer music. All my efforts are aimed at broadening the meaning and understanding of contemporary classical music. In my works I try to convey what I find inspiring for myself in inter-dependencies and order in nature, math and physics.
I truly believe all of this is about much more than just something that happens in the air.
As Ernst Levy said: "It is something that, first and last, happens in the soul. To an inner, spiritual something coresponds an outer, physical something: tone. Music happens when both are 'attuned' to each other." It is up to me to make these two worlds meet.
