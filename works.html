2018

Birds Still Sing

The composition was created as a response to a book with the same title I read during my trip to the countryside. This piece is part of a miniature series for solo instruments. The recording comes from the premiere during the concert in Copenhagen.
Performing: tenor sax - Lucas Bjerg
Recording: Maheen Adamson, Chanice Reeves

2017

Desert Steps

Soundtrack for new CGI animated short film, yet to be released, made by the independent film company, Fudge Park.
Performing: Simple Ensemble, Jacob Kim-Lee

2020

Kepler-1649c

I dedicated this piece for voice and computer to the astronomer, Felix McGee who is also a friend of mine. I performed it with the singer, Akemi Sato and recorded it in my own home studio. I used the samples of Kepler-1649c electro-magnetic waves converted into the sound made by NASA. All of the sounds are highly transposed and transformed in various ways.
Performing: soprano - Akemi Sato, Jacob Kim-Lee
Recording: Jacob Kim-Lee

2019

Scary One

A piece commissioned by a contemporary theater group called "Never Newer" from Dresden. The music is the soundtrack for a theater play about tough emotions.
Performing: Augenblick Ensemble

2019

Windy Though

This piece is an effect of the cooperation with the Greek composer, Thalis Logoulis on Contemporary Music Festival in Athens. It is a recording of improvisation with the use of the piano and plenty of digital techniques.
Performing: Thalis Logoulis, Jacob Kim-Lee
